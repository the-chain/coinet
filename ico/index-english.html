DAICO {CFP}

It is an improvement in the ICO fundraising model that incorporates certain aspects of DAO. The idea was suggested by Vitalik Buterin in January 2018 and aims to make the ICO safer by involving investors in the initial process of project development. It will also allow the tokens' holders to vote for the refund of the contributed funds if they are not happy with the progress made by the developers.

For projects that implement the concept DAICO, it will force a level of responsibility on the part of the developers and give the token holders more peace of mind that they are guaranteed to see at least a minimum viable product or recover their money.

https://cointelegraph.com/explained/what-is-a-daico-explained

COINET believes in self-regulation and in the technological systems that allow it to be executed. We have not done anything until now if third party regulations limit mechanisms with as much potential for the development and advance of projects as the ICO. CFP has the spirit of DAICO and intends to put control in the hands of investors to completely mitigate the risk of ICO scams where developers make a symbolic sale and then escape with the money as soon as the ICO ends, without producing no product

The projects that are on the COINET platform must accept compliance with the conditions or measures applied in the DAICO. The signing of the documents and the presentation of the smart contract will be on the platform so that investors through the Proof and Source system can vote and guarantee its execution.
